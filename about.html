2023 All About Me

My name is Brenna and I am a student working to expand my photography abilities. I took a semester of photography last year and I am looking forward to picking it up again this year. I enjoy being behind a camera and working to capture a piece of art.

Besides photography, I am interested in reading, dance, and music. I play the piano, flute, bassoon, and guitar. I am also in a folk dance group that specializes in traditional American dancing and clogging. I joined last summer and got to tour in Spain with the group. Some of my life goals include traveling the world and surviving high school.
